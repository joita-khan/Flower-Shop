About Flowers

A flower, sometimes known as a bloom or blossom, is the
reproductive structure found in flowering plants
(plants of the division Angiospermae). Flowers produce gametophytes,
which in flowering plants consist of a few haploid cells which
produce gametes. The "male" gametophyte, which produces non-motilebr
sperm, is enclosed within pollen grains; the "female" gametophyte is contained within the ovule.
